Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes! All Luxe Cosmetics products are 100% cruelty-free and Leaping Bunny certified. We never test on animals.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unopened products. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Are your products vegan?

Most of our products are vegan. Look for the vegan badge on product pages, or check the ingredient list.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express and overnight options are available at checkout.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email.
